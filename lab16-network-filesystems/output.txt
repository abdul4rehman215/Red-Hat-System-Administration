===== TASK 2: Mount NFS Share =====

/share *

$ sudo mount -t nfs 10.0.2.20:/share /mnt/nfs_share

$ df -hT | grep nfs

===== TASK 3: Mount CIFS Share =====

$ ls -l /etc/cifs_credentials
-rw------- 1 root root 42 Feb 22 17:12 /etc/cifs_credentials

$ sudo mount -t cifs -o credentials=/etc/cifs_credentials \
  //10.0.2.30/shared /mnt/cifs_share

$ df -hT | grep cifs
//10.0.2.30/shared   cifs 100G   5.0G   95G   5%  /mnt/cifs_share

$ ls /mnt/cifs_share
reports
team_folder
windows_docs
