------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Verify Shell
------------------------------------------------------------

-bash-4.2$ echo $SHELL

/bin/bash


------------------------------------------------------------
Step 2: Create Lab Directory
------------------------------------------------------------

-bash-4.2$ mkdir job_control_lab && cd job_control_lab

(Current directory: /home/centos/job_control_lab)


------------------------------------------------------------
Task 1: Starting Background Processes
------------------------------------------------------------

-bash-4.2$ sleep 300 &

[1] 18472

Explanation:
[1]     → Job number
18472   → Process ID (PID)


Verify Background Job:

-bash-4.2$ jobs

[1]+  Running                 sleep 300 &


------------------------------------------------------------
Task 2: Managing Job States
------------------------------------------------------------

Bring Job to Foreground:

-bash-4.2$ fg %1

sleep 300


Suspend Process:
(User presses Ctrl + Z)

^Z
[1]+  Stopped                 sleep 300


Resume in Background:

-bash-4.2$ bg %1

[1]+ sleep 300 &


Verify:

-bash-4.2$ jobs

[1]+  Running                 sleep 300 &


------------------------------------------------------------
Task 3: Process Termination
------------------------------------------------------------

List Jobs with PID:

-bash-4.2$ jobs -l

[1]+ 18472 Running                 sleep 300 &


Graceful Termination:

-bash-4.2$ kill 18472

(No immediate output)


Verify:

-bash-4.2$ jobs

[1]+  Terminated              sleep 300


-bash-4.2$ jobs

(No active jobs remaining.)


------------------------------------------------------------
Force Termination Demonstration
------------------------------------------------------------

-bash-4.2$ sleep 200 &

[1] 18503


-bash-4.2$ kill -9 18503


Verify:

-bash-4.2$ jobs

[1]+  Killed                  sleep 200
